Our proposal. How it works?

Humboldt Brasil has developed a Global Platform for PES - Payment for Environmental Services, capable of monetizing the forests positive externalities. Thus landowners can be remunerated and projects of conversation or reforestation well financed

1

Identification of the area, environmental inventory and contracting with the owner

2

Issuance of representative titles / certificates of conservation rights for environmental assets

3

Fundraising

4

PES payment flow for environmental landowners and agents

About Us

We are a startup that aims to monetize forest assets through a (global) platform for transactions between owners and investors, among other potential users. The project has the potential to preserve 100 million hectares, reforest 175 million hectares in Brazil and move US $ 50 billion per year, which demonstrates the size of its economic, social and environmental impacts. We have support of Grupo Cornélio Brennand and Amazon Web Services Activate startup program. Validated the MVP and are now completing the first production-ready version of the platform.
